TUI:Scripts:Built In Scripts:DIS:Focus

The DIS:Focus script allows to you take a series of exposures over a range of secondary mirror focus values, reports the resulting FWHMs and optionally adjusts the focus to the estimated best value.

The sequence is:

  • Move the boresight so the focus star is visible next to the slit.
  • Take an exposure (at the current focus) to verify there's a usable star. Note that the FWHM of this star is ignored; this first image just helps with acquisition.
  • If no star is found, the script pauses while you acquire the star and/or improve focus enough that it can be centroided.
  • Take a sequence of exposures at varying focus and measure FWHM of the star at each focus.
  • Fit the resulting FWHMs to a parabola to find the best focus.
  • Optionally move the focus to the best-fit focus (if one can be found) and take another exposure.
  • At the end, the boresight is always restored to its original position (even if the script is aborted or fails).
