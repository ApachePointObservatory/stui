TUI:Scripts:Built In Scripts:NICFPS:Data Cube

The NICFPS:Data Cube script allows you to take a sequence of exposures over a range of wavelengths. You specify:

  • The number of exposures per wavelength
  • The beginning etalon z spacing
  • The ending etalon z spacing
  • The number of wavelengths at which to expose. 2 is the smallest allowed value and will expose at just the beginning and ending etalon spacings.

The etalon spacings are traversed in two interleaved passes:

  1. beg z, beg z + 2 * delta z, beg z + 4 * delta z, ...
  2. beg z + delta z, beg z + 3 * delta z...

Warning: this script does not restore the etalon z spacing to its original value when done. So NICFPS will almost certainly have a different spacing after you run this script.
