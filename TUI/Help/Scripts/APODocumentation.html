TUI:Scripts:APO Documentation

Before you can command an instrument, the TCC or the hub, you need to know its commands and its replies to those commands. Documentation is served at APO.

Craig Loomis maintains a wonderful page on operations software. This is the first place to look if you are interested in commands and replies from any instrument, the TCC or the hub.

For more general information about an instrument, try the excellent search funtion on APO's home page or browse Telescope & Instrument Information.
