TUI:Guiding:Echelle Slitviewer

The Echelle Slitviewer window controls the echelle slitviewer. Most of the controls are standard guiding controls. However, the controls that adjust the filter are unique to the Echelle slitviewer. These appear in a box just above the status bar:

  • Filter the current/desired filter.
  • Apply apply the requested filter change.
  • Current restore the filter pop-up menu to the current filter.
