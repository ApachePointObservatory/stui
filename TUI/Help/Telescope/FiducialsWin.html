STUI:Telescope:Fiducials window

A log of fidicial crossings for the azimuth, altitude and rotator axes. At this time only values that are received after you connect are shown. The columns are:

  • TAI Time: the date at which the keyword was seen by STUI
  • Ind: the index of the fiducial
  • Pos: the nominal position of the fiducial (degrees)
  • Error: the error in reported position (ticks)

Note: the "error since last crossing" value (the second-to-last value of the keyword) is not displayed.
