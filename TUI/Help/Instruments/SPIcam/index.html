TUI:Instruments:SPIcam

Controls for SPIcam, the Seaver Prototype Imaging camera, are distributed among two windows:

  • The SPIcam window displays status and allows configuring SPIcam. The status portion is always displayed. The configuration controls are hidden by default.
  • The SPIcam Expose window controls and displays exposure sequences.

See also the SPIcam documentation at APO.
