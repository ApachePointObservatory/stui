TUI:Instruments:NICFPS

Controls for NICFPS are distributed among two windows:

  • The NICFPS window displays status and allows configuring NICFPS. The status portion is always displayed. The configuration controls are hidden by default.
  • The NICFPS Expose window controls and displays exposure sequences. Note: you can stop a NICFPS exposure but you cannot pause or abort one. Thus the Abort button is missing and the Pause button will pause the exposure sequence after the current exposure finishes.

See also the NICFPS documentation at APO.
