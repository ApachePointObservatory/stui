TUI:Misc:Truss Lamps window

Control the truss lamps: calibration lamps mounted on the secondary cage. Press a button to immediately toggle the state of the associated lamp. A "!" will be displayed until the lamp has changed state.

Warning: as of 2004-10-01, there are multiple ways to control the truss lamps that do not communicate with each other. As a result, the displayed state in the Truss Lamps window may be wrong. This is PR 63 and will be fixed at APO.

Notes:

  • If the lamp controls say "Rebooting" then you should wait until the tlamps controller finishes rebooting.
  • If a lamp control says "Unknown", you can still try to toggle the lamp, but it is likely that the lamp controller needs some help.
