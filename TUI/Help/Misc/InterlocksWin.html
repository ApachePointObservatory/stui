STUI:Misc:Interlocks Window

Display system interlocks.

You may click on an interlock button (red or green square) to bring up more information about it.

Various other windows can be opened using the pop-up menu at the lower corner of the Interlocks window:

  • All Interlocks: a list of every interlock and its current state. You can get help on this window by pressing the Help button along the bottom.
  • System Status: brings up a "cartoon" of the system; this is a very efficient way to display the state of each interlock, but the items are not labelled.
  • Ignored Interlocks: I'm not sure what this does.

Note: this window and its subwindows are taken directly from the old SDSS control system. As such, they have a different look and feel than normal STUI windows.
